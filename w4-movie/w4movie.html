Master of the Devil Cult (魔道祖师, Módào Zǔshī; or: Founder of Diabolism) is an animated adaptation of the Chinese xianxia novel of the same name. The original story was written by Mòxiāng Tóngxiù (墨香铜臭). The first season aired on Tencent Video from July 9, 2018 to October 6, 2018, while the second season aired from August 3, 2019 to August 31, 2019. A third season was confirmed in the final episode of the second season, and the chibi series is due out in 2020. The animation won the Gold Award for Best Serial Animation at the 15th China Animation Golden Dragon Awards (第十五届中国动漫金龙奖). It also won "Best New Animation" for the Xingang Award (新光奖); 7th China Xi'an International Original Animation Competition Xuanzheng (玄正) 20, Gengchen (庚辰) - Elder Yiling, Wei WuXian's debut in the Sun Annihilation War, the end of the Wen clan.

As a magic caster, the founder of the Devil Cult, Wei WuXian wandered the world on his "crooked path", hated by millions for the chaos he created. In the end, he was stabbed in the back by his dearest Sheedy and was killed by powerful orders that banded together to overpower him. He incarnates in the body of a madman, who is kicked out of the order, and later, unwittingly, meets the famous spellcaster among the orders - Lan Wangji, his sworn enemy. This marks the start of an exciting yet fun journey of attacking monsters, solving mysteries and raising children. Through mutual flirting along the way, Wei WuXian gradually realizes that Lan Wangji, an outwardly arrogant and indifferent impassive person, has more feelings for Wei WuXian than he lets on.

Orders

Gusu Lan

Gusu Lan (姑苏蓝氏, Gusu Lan Shì) is a clan founded by Lan An, whose seat is in the secluded mountains.
“Fog almost always shrouded the local white walls and black tiled roofs of houses stretching along picturesque gardens with gazebos near the water, creating a picture of a cloudy ocean in the realm of the immortals. At dawn, the first rays of the morning sun broke through the clouds of fog floating everywhere and everywhere, which fully justified the name of this settlement - Cloud Depths.



Qishan Wen

The Qishan Wen Sect (岐山温氏, Qíshān Wēn Shì) is one of the Grand Orders, now defunct. The seat of the order was the Nightless City in Qishan. Its last head was Wen Ruohan.



Lanling Jin

The Lanling Jin Clan (兰陵金氏, Lánlíng Jīn Shì) is one of the Four Great Orders. The seat of the Order is the Koi Tower in Lanling.
The current head of the Order is Jin Rulan.



Qinghe Nie

The Qinghe Nie Sect (清河聂氏, Qīnghé Niè Shì) is one of the Grand Orders based in the Qinghe region. His residence is the Unclean Yudol.



Yunmeng Jiang

The Yunmeng Jiang Sect (云梦江氏, Yúnmèng Jiāng Shì) is one of the Grand Orders. The seat of the Order is Lotus Pier, located in Yunmeng.
The current leader is Jiang Cheng.



Demonic Cultination

Taoism is the doctrine of dao (道) or "the way of things", a Chinese traditional teaching that includes elements of religion and philosophy.
At the heart of the system of Taoism is the concept of "dao" ("way") - the beginning, impersonal world law, the way of comprehending nature and its laws. Tao is nothing, the beginning and end of the world, since all material things are born from non-existence, and then, being destroyed, again go into non-existence.
