The Grandmaster of Demonic Cultination ( Mo Dao Zu Shi ), this is a beautiful anime with a very deep, intricate and interesting plot. It is very exciting to watch the development of the plot and all the characters in general. Each character that appears on the screen has a background and the author has revealed them all very much, and the story itself will affect even the coldest people.
I recommend this anime and give it a 10/10 rating. Join us and let's follow the adventures of Wei Wuxian and Lan Jan together.
